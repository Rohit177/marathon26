Read Carefully

Rules & Guidelines

BIB Non-Transferable

Your assigned BIB number is unique to you and cannot be transferred to another runner under any circumstances.

Follow Official Route

Participants must stay on the designated course at all times. Taking shortcuts will result in disqualification.

Prohibited Items

Bicycles, skates, motorized vehicles, tobacco, alcohol, or banned substances are strictly prohibited at the venue and on the route.

Medical Safety

Run at your own risk. If you feel unwell, dizzy, or experience pain during the race, stop immediately and alert the nearest volunteer.
